PAPER TO PAPERLINK IN MINUTES!

Set Up

1

Go to Welcome email, click on PWA Business Page and open it.

2

Click on the download icon on upper right browser, app icon should be on your screen.

3

Open PWA Business Page

Set Up

3

From the Business Page, click on each file and test how your clients will experience a Confirm, Sign, and Complete file.

4

Check your email and you will see a PDF copy the files you just tested.

Manage Files

5

Log into your Paperlink Console, Click Paperlink Pages, then + icon to upload a file.

6

File Manager is opened, select Complete, Confirm, or Sign action. In Complete or Sign, place stickers to make it easier for end user.

6.1

Stickers > Place these stickers where needed. Paperlink will auto fill those lines so end user can finish file faster.

6.2

Privacy > Public by default, a link that everyone can access. Private is a link that users have to ask for permission to access.Do Not Post, Paperlink will appear in back office, but will not appear on Business Page.

6.3

Set Action, privacy, place all necessary stickers, then click on Publish Our Unique API platform will create a Paperlink file.
